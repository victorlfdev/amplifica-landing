# Design System: Cinematic Obsidian ## Brand: Amplifica Mídias --- ## 1. Creative Vision & North Star The "Cinematic Obsidian" design system is engineered to reflect the precision, depth, and authority of a high-end audiovisual production house. It draws heavy inspiration from **Apple's High-End aesthetic**: minimalist, functional, and deeply immersive. **The North Star:** *Precision through Simplicity.* Every pixel serves the visual narrative. --- ## 2. Visual Language ### 2.1 Color Palette Focused on depth and luxury, using a "Dark Mode" foundation to make visual content pop. - **Deep Obsidian (#0A0A0A):** The primary background. Provides a limitless, cinematic feel. - **Muted Platinum (#E5E5E7):** Primary typography. Soft enough to prevent eye strain but sharp enough for high legibility. - **Soft Gold (#D5B98A):** Accent color. Used sparingly for high-value CTAs and subtle highlights to denote "Premium" service. - **Glass/Translucent:** Used for navigation bars and overlays to maintain a sense of depth and layering. ### 2.2 Typography - **Primary Font:** *Manrope* - **Headlines:** SemiBold/Bold with tight letter-spacing for a modern, authoritative look. - **Body:** Regular with generous line-height (1.6) to ensure breathability and elegance. - **Scale:** - Hero Display: 80px - 110px - Section Headers: 48px - 64px - Body Text: 18px - 20px ### 2.3 Grid & Spacing - **The "Breathe" Principle:** Generous use of whitespace (negative space) to focus the user's attention on the portfolio pieces. - **12-Column Grid:** Standard for desktop layout, ensuring alignment and structural integrity. - **Vertical Rhythm:** Large padding (120px - 200px) between sections to create a sense of scale. --- ## 3. Component Library ### 3.1 Navigation (Glassmorphism) - **Style:** Floating navbar with a blur effect (`backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`). - **Behavior:** Stays fixed at the top with a subtle transition as the user scrolls. ### 3.2 Buttons (Premium Tech) - **Primary:** Soft Gold background with dark text. Subtle 4px corner radius. - **Secondary:** Outlined Platinum. - **Interactions:** Scale up by 2% on hover with a slow transition (300ms) and a soft glow effect. ### 3.3 Cards & Containers - **Border:** 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1). - **Background:** Subtle dark gradients to give a 3D feel. --- ## 4. Interaction Design & Motion ### 4.1 Scroll-Driven Animations - **Reveal:** Elements fade in and slide up slightly (20px) as they enter the viewport. - **Scale:** Video containers expand slightly as they reach the center of the screen to create an "immersive focus." ### 4.2 Transitions - All page transitions are smooth (600ms+) to mimic the feel of a high-end cinematic edit. - Micro-interactions on links: Underline expands from center on hover. ### 4.3 Parallax Effects (New) - **Subtle Depth:** Using different scroll speeds for background images and foreground text to create a sense of 3D space. - **Hero Parallax:** The background video/image in the Hero section moves at a slower rate (0.5x) than the scroll, creating a 'window' effect into the production world. - **Service Cards:** Images within the service sections have a slight internal parallax movement (vertical shift) as they enter the viewport, adding a layer of tactile realism. - **Floating Accents:** Subtle geometric or light-leak elements that float at different layers to enhance the cinematic depth and luxury feel. --- ## 5. Screen Strategy: The Landing Page Flow 1. **Hero Section:** - Full-screen 4K background video (muted, loop) with a 0.5x parallax scroll effect. - Centered, high-impact headline: "Crafting Audiovisual Masterpieces." - Minimalist scroll indicator. 2. **The Narrative (Intro):** - Brief, bold statement about the brand’s philosophy. 3. **Services (The Verticals):** - **Social Media Strategy:** Visual-first layout showing high-performance content with internal image parallax. - **Event Coverage:** Wide, immersive imagery showing scale and energy. - **Post-Production:** Split-screen "Before/After" or detail-oriented focus on editing/color grading. 4. **Portfolio Gallery:** - A masonry or clean grid of the best work, appearing as the user scrolls. 5. **Conversion (CTA):** - A minimalist contact section: "Let's Start a Project."
